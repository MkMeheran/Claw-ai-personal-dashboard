## 🧠 &nbsp; What is NEXUS?

> NEXUS replaces five or more fragmented subscription apps with **one unified, self-hosted system** — running on your accounts, in your database, under your control.

Most productivity tools share three critical problems:

- They charge you **monthly, forever** — even for tools you built your life around
- They store your data **on their servers, under their terms** — not yours
- When they shut down or raise prices, **you lose everything you built**

NEXUS solves all three at once.

It is not a theoretical product. It was engineered by its creator and is **used daily as a personal command center** — a single system that handles clipboard management, private media storage, AI assistance, encrypted credentials, focus sessions, analytics, and a full knowledge base — all in one place, all on your own infrastructure.

Built once. Configured to your workflow. Handed over with complete source code. **You own it forever.**

## ⚙️ &nbsp; Core Features

| &nbsp; | Feature | What it does |
|:---:|---|---|
| 📋 | **Clipboard Sync** | Universal clipboard that captures and syncs copied content **instantly** across Windows and Android. Text, links, and snippets available on both devices the moment you copy — no manual steps. |
| 🔒 | **Media Vault** | Private, encrypted personal storage for photos, videos, and files. No third-party cloud. No automatic uploads to corporate servers. Your files live in **your Supabase storage bucket**. |
| 🤖 | **AI Assistant** | A custom-integrated personal AI layer built directly into the NEXUS interface. Handles daily queries, task summarization, note synthesis, and contextual productivity support. |
| 🔐 | **Encrypted Notes & Password Vault** | End-to-end encrypted storage for credentials, sensitive notes, and private knowledge base entries. Structured for both quick retrieval and long-term archiving. |
| 🎯 | **Focus Tracker** | Pomodoro-style deep work session tracker with session logging and streak tracking. Designed to build **sustainable focus habits** — not just count minutes. |
| 📊 | **Personal Analytics Dashboard** | Visual productivity metrics showing daily output patterns, habit consistency, and system usage. Your data, visible to **you** — not anonymized and sent to a company. |
| 🧠 | **Personal Knowledge Base** | A searchable second-brain system for reference material, ideas, research notes, and project documentation. Everything indexed and retrievable in seconds. |
| 🔄 | **Cross-Device Real-Time Sync** | All data synchronizes live between the Windows desktop app and Android mobile app via **Supabase Realtime**. No manual sync. No delays. |
